Jagannanna Vidya Deevana

About the Program

Jagannanna Vidya Deevana is a scholarship program initiated by the government to provide financial assistance to eligible students pursuing higher education.

Benefits

  • Full or partial fee reimbursement.
  • Provision of textbooks and study materials.
  • Hostel and mess fee waivers for eligible students.
  • Other benefits...

Application Process

  1. Visit the official website of Jagannanna Vidya Deevana.
  2. Register as a new user or log in with your existing credentials.
  3. Fill out the application form with accurate details.
  4. Upload the required documents, such as educational certificates and income proof.
  5. Submit the application form online.
  6. Track the application status on the website.
  7. If approved, follow the instructions provided to avail the benefits.
