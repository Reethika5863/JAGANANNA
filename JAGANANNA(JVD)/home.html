Jagannanna Vidya Deevena

  • Jagannanna Vidya Deevena is a welfare scheme introduced by the Government of Andhra Pradesh, India. It aims to provide financial assistance to students pursuing higher education in professional courses.
  • Under this scheme, eligible students receive financial support to cover their tuition fees, mess charges, and other academic expenses. The scheme's objective is to ensure that no student faces financial constraints in pursuing their education.
  • To avail the benefits of Jagannanna Vidya Deevena, students need to fulfill certain eligibility criteria set by the government. They must be residents of Andhra Pradesh, pursuing professional courses in recognized institutions, and their family's annual income should be below a specified threshold.
  • The scheme has brought relief to numerous students, allowing them to focus on their studies without worrying about financial burdens. It has played a crucial role in promoting higher education and enabling students to pursue their dreams.
