Jagananna Vidya Deevana Registration Form

  • Anyone in the family availing of the pension will not eligible for the scheme.
  • No one of the family members is a government employee is only eligible for the scheme.
  • The students who are studying all courses covered under the scheme are eligible.
  • The families who are below the poverty line are only eligible.
  • The family head income should be under Rs. 2.5 lakh per anum.
  • The family shouldn’t own a four-wheel vehicle i.e car, auto, etc…
  • Anyone in the family who pays poverty tax or IT returns are not eligible for the scheme.
  • The family having less than 1500 sq. ft house is eligible for the scheme.
  • The family who is availing of the ration are eligible for the scheme.
