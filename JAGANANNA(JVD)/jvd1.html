Welcome to Jagananna Vidya Deevana

Jagananna Vidya Deevana is a scholarship program aimed at providing financial assistance to students for their education.

Key Features:

    1. Free education for eligible students.
    2. Financial support for textbooks and uniforms.
    3. Hostel facility for students from economically weaker sections.
    4. Transportation assistance for students.
